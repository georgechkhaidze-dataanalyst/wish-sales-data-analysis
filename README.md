# Wish Summer Sales - Advanced Data Analytics 📊

## 🎯 Project Objective
This project is a comprehensive business intelligence study of the Wish e-commerce platform. Using a dataset of **1,341 products**, I performed a full-cycle analysis to identify the exact drivers of sales growth and merchant success.

## 💡 Key Business Insights & "Winning Formula"
Through deep-dive SQL querying and Python processing, I uncovered the **"Perfect Product Profile"** that leads to a **5.8x increase in sales** compared to average listings:
- **The Ideal Price Point:** Products priced between **$5 - $10** see the highest conversion density.
- **Shipping Strategy:** Keeping shipping costs between **$1 - $3** is a critical success factor; Express shipping did not show a significant ROI.
- **The Trust Stack:** Merchant ratings (**4.0 - 4.49**) and social proof (**1,000+ reviews**) are stronger sales drivers than deep discounts.
- **Color Dominance:** Neutral colors (Black, White, Blue) account for over **50% of total units sold**.

## 🕵️ Advanced Analytics & Anomaly Detection
- **Pricing Inconsistency:** Identified that in **35.6% of cases**, the listing price was higher than the retail price, suggesting data entry errors or strategic pricing overlaps.
- **Outlier Analysis:** Isolated **15 anomalous products** that achieved high sales (50,000+ units) despite not following standard market trends, providing insights into niche high-performers.
- **Ad Boost Efficiency:** My analysis showed that the `ad_boost` feature has a marginal or even negative impact on high-selling products, suggesting a need for budget reallocation.

## 🛠 Technical Stack
- **SQL (MS SQL Server):** Used complex joins, CTEs, and CASE statements for data segmentation and "Perfect Product" modeling.
- **Python (Pandas/Jupyter):** Handled data cleaning, missing value imputation, and initial EDA.
- **Power BI:** Built an interactive 4-page dashboard to visualize sales drivers and merchant performance.
